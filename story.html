Our Story

Our story is simple. We met back in 2008 at William S. Fort Memorial Park. Alex would regularly drive down to the park to pick up his cousin from school. Brandee, a junior at the time, was initially struck by Alex’s adorableness. She liked his passive, yet goofy personality. He thought she was down-right cute and loved her sense of humor.

By December they went on their first date together to see Iron Man and were official by the 23rd! From then on they would spend countless afternoons together playing Wii games and watching lots and lots of movies. They’ve been inseparable ever since.

Fast forward 6 years and here we are! Engaged and loving it! We are so excited to start our lives together and are even more excited to share this special day with all our loved ones. Here’s to married life and many fun adventures
